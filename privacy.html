Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Tier Axis LLC ("TierAxis," "we," "us," or "our") is an Oklahoma limited liability company. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle information when you visit our website at TierAxis.com or contact us through our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you submit our contact form. This includes your first and last name, work email address, company name, and the details of the initiative or inquiry you share with us. We do not collect payment information, sensitive personal data, or any information through automated tracking tools beyond standard web server logs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to respond to your inquiry, assess whether and how TierAxis can help with your initiative, and communicate with you about our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Store Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is received and stored by the TierAxis team. We retain your information only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain records of our business communications. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies for advertising or analytics purposes at this time. Standard web server logs may record your IP address, browser type, and pages visited as part of normal website operation.

Your Choices

If you would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of information you have submitted to us, you may contact us at info@TierAxis.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for business professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at info@TierAxis.com.
